## Critical Issue: Site Not Indexed

As of March 2026, `site:daytoanthaylong.com` returns **zero results** on Google. The site is invisible to search engines.

### Immediate Actions Required

1. **Google Search Console** — Register at https://search.google.com/search-console
   - Verify domain ownership (DNS TXT record recommended)
   - Submit sitemap: `https://daytoanthaylong.com/sitemap.xml`
   - Use URL Inspection → "Request Indexing" for the homepage
2. **Google Business Profile** — Create/claim at https://business.google.com
   - Business name: "Dạy Toán Thầy Long"
   - Category: "Trung tâm dạy kèm" / "Tutoring Service"
   - Add photos (room1, room2, portrait)
   - Add operating hours, phone numbers, website link
   - Video verification may be required (2026 standard)
3. **Bing Webmaster Tools** — Submit at https://www.bing.com/webmasters
4. **Submit to Vietnamese directories** — Foody.vn, Yellowpages.vn, local education directories
